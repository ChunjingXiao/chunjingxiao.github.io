I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in Chengdu, China. Currently, I hold the position of an Associate Professor at the School of Computer and Information Engineering, Henan University in Kaifeng, China. During my academic journey, I had the privilege of being a visiting scholar with the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Northwestern University, located in Evanston, IL, USA. My research interests lie in the fields of recommender systems, anomaly detection, and the Internet of Things. These areas have captivated my passion, and I am constantly seeking ways to contribute to their advancements. As an academic and researcher, I am dedicated to exploring innovative solutions and making meaningful contributions to these domains. More information about my experience and research can be found on Google Scholar.
